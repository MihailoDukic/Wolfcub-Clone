Developing luxury for established innovators

Cloisall is a leading UAE based interiors and fit-out contractor operating in the mid- to high-end luxury space.

Cloisall has an excellent reputation for their innovative work and has worked on high profile projects in locations including Dubai International Airport, UAE University, Ferrari World and Dubai Mall Metro Link Bridge to name just a few.

Purpose

Adur & Worthing approached us to help prototype a radical new digital repairs service. The current systems were antiquated and they wanted to offer a more innovative, digital product to residents.

Challenges

The project involved four different and unique service areas: self-service, customer service, scheduling and workforce. With a multitude of stakeholders across these departments and services, our biggest challenge was capturing their needs and feeding that into the design of the product.

Our Approach

We used our initial research to plan out and map each service area in full. This allowed us to get feedback and validate our findings and ensured the structure of the application was relevant to the audience’s needs.

We worked closely with Adur & Worthing's product team, having fortnightly sprints to keep things on track.

CREATIVE

Building a style

We used our initial research and created an extensive set of brand guidelines.


This guide was then used as the foundation for rolling out marketing collateral and for digital applications.

The Result

Our final task was to complete the rollout of a new responsive website which showcases Cloisall’s excellent work in an understated yet bold way. The goal was to keep their work as the focus while adding elements of quality and style within the design.

DESIGN

Building a showcase

Cloisall has an excellent client base and it was our task to help showcase their achievements across the project pages on the site.



We created a story-driven projects builder which allowed Cloisall to share what makes their projects unique in an innovative way.
